The School Outreach Project


Project Overview

The school outreach project aims to help the community around us by educated the youth of Columbus on programming basics. School outreach meets once a week to work on modules designed to faciliate the student's learning of several programming concepts. These modules can be games, activities, or anything else you can think of! We, then, take these modules to local schools and educate the students with our workshops.
