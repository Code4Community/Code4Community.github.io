Mental Health Web App Project


Project Overview

This is a web application designed to support people with mental health issues. Users will log onto the website and see a personalized dashboard where they can view resources related to mental health, life skills, and well-being. They can also take two different surveys which measure levels of depression and anxiety respectively. After taking a survey, the website will provide resources tailored specifically to the user. This may include websites, apps, music, games, and movies among other suggestions.

Current State of the Project

We are using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node) for our web application. Thus far, we have implemented the database schema, linked the front end to the back end, nearly completed the survey code, and created a page structure.
