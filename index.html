Our Mission

At Code 4 Community, our mission is to help our community through computer science. This can include teaching computer science to grade school students, working with researchers to develop applications to help those with mental illness, and much more! If you are interested in helping others through code, then we are the club for you!

FAQ

  • Do I need to know how to code?

    The short answer is no. Though it helps to know a programming language or two (because it helps you to learn new programming languages faster), you do not need to know how to code. Through the programming tutorials we go through at the begginning of the fall semester, self study, and learning by doing, you will be able to contribute to the projects.

    Everyone, if they know how to code or not, will learn something new through Code 4 Community, so come join us to learn something new and help others!

  • How much of a time commitment is Code 4 Community?

    We have one 1.5 hour meeting per week. Moreover, if you participate in school outreach, we have occasional outreach events that are generally about two hours long.

  • I have more questions! Where do I ask them?

    You can email us at c4cosu@gmail.com to ask any questions you may have.
